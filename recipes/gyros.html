Gyros Recipe

The gyro is a classic Greek dish, with the meat traditionally cooked on a vertical spit and shaved off in individual servings

  • 1 pound ground lamb, or beef
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup minced red onion
  • 2 teaspoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Oil as needed
  1. To make the meat mixture, throughly combine the ground lamb, salt, black pepper, cumin, nutmeg, oregano, garlic, red onion and lemon juice in a mixing bowl
  2. Divide into four equal portions and shape into oblong patties about 3-inch wide by 6-inch long by 1/2-inch thick
  3. Preheat a grill or pan over high heat
  4. brush the grill or grill pan with oil
